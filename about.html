About me: from then to now

Hello there. My name is Ore Arowobusoye. I have been playing piano since I was a little girl and I have a real passion for music.

Currently, I have completed up to level 7 with the canadian royal conservatory of music, and received honours on my level 7 exam. I first started teaching piano with a program called heart of the city that offers free music lessons for low income families. These days I am teaching my own students with my piano at home .
