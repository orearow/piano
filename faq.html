Frequently asked questions

How much do lessons cost?

Lessons cost $15 per half hour ($30 per hour)

Will you come to my house

Unfortunately no, I do not travel, all lessons are at my house

Can I bring my own book?

Unfortunately no, I follow a specific curriculum

Can I bring a specific song to learn

Yes! As long as the song is do-able for your level range

Do you teach lessons in the summer?

No, I teach lessons as per the academic calender. Sept-June (with a winter break)

Will I have to play in front of people?

Yes, there are two recitals. A christmas recital and an end of year recital. Closer to these dates I will supply you with more information
